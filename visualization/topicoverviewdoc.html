What you see

This is the first view on loading the visualization. It shows the k topics requested at run time, in order of prevalence in the corpus.

How it is determined

Topics are identified by the top three words taken from the --output-topic-keys MALLET output file (topics.tsv).

Lengths are determined by the sum of weights for each topic according to the --output-doc-topics MALLET output file (docComp.tsv). Lengths are then scaled to the most prevalent.
