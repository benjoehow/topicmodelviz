What you see

Details on a specific topic.

Words are shown on the right, ordered by association of word with the topic.

Documents are shown in the middle of the view based on the individual document's association with the topic of interest.

How it is determined

Topics are identified by the top three words taken from the --output-topic-keys MALLET output file (topics.tsv). Words are taken from same file.

Documents are ordered based on the weight value from the --output-doc-topics MALLET output file (docComp.tsv). Lengths are then scaled to the most prevalent.

D3 Method (topicmodelviz.js)

topicviz(id) where id is the topic id assigned by MALLET
