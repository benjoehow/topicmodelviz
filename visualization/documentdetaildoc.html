What you see

Details on a specific document.

Association with topics are shown on the left, lengths are bar are scaled to highest association.

Documents content is shown in the middle.

How it is determined

Topics are identified by the top three words taken from the --output-topic-keys MALLET output file (topics.tsv).

Topic lengths are ordered based on the weight value from the --output-doc-topics MALLET output file (docComp.tsv). Lengths are then scaled to the most prevalent.

Document content is pulled from the native file in the corpus directory.

D3 Method (topicmodelviz.js)

docviz(id) where id is the base name of the document.
